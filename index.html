Analysis of Gifford Pinchot's 1913 Congressional Testimony about the Hetch Hetchy Dam

Ishaan Chaturvedi · English 1102 · Dr. Young · April 23, 2026

About This Exhibit

In December 1913, Gifford Pinchot testified before Congress in support of the Raker Act — legislation that would authorize the flooding of Hetch Hetchy Valley inside Yosemite National Park.1 His testimony did not win by overwhelming the opposition with evidence. It won by changing the terms of the debate entirely, making only one conclusion seem reasonable before any real argument had been made.
